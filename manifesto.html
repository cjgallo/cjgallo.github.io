Hot dogs and eggs

What's in a name?


Most people claim their own name as a domain. My namesakes at Ernest and Julio Gallo Winery have commandeered domains that include the word "gallo" in them.

So when I started this site a few years ago, I was stumped on a domain name. And then I heard my grandma talking about making hot dogs and eggs for me as a kid. I kept saying it all day. Hot dogs and eggs. It made me smile :)

I bought the domain name and laughed. But it stuck. It's goofy and different, just like me. A curious combination, hot dogs and eggs.

The name has come to mean much more to me. It's part of my philosophy. And it represents what I believe.
