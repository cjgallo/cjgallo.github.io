I'm Chris Gallo.


And this is hot dogs and eggs. It's my little piece of the world wide web. A place for me to stay curious.

I spend my days working with 7 lovely people at Highrise. We make software that helps you track who you talk to, so that you're prepared to do business. My job is to make the people that use Highrise feel important.

I write to think on my blog. My writing is published in other places on the web like Signal v. Noise and The Secondary Break (I'm a big UNC basketball fan).

Every so often, I transcribe interesting interviews and talks. It helps me share what I learn from people I admire, and it's a great resource for others too. Two of my favorites are Manoj Bhargava and Julie Zhuo.

I love to try to make things with computers too. Here are a few static sites I've put together.

  • cbbstatshelp.com: learn about advanced analytics for college basketball.
  • dadgumboxscores.com: find any UNC men's basketball box score since 2003-2004.
  • dalecarnegieismyhomeboy.com: all 30 principles from How to Win Friends and Influence People
