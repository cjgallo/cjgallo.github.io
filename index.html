It's food for thought,
hot dogs and eggs

A curious combination of beliefs, hot dogs and eggs is a simple recipe.

Listen

Because the more you listen, the more you will learn. Don't just listen to what people say, listen to what people do.

Ask questions

Never underestimate the power of a question. Because you can't get what you don't ask for.

Do less

Because when you try to do everything, you're good at nothing. Focus is saying no. Stay small. And grow slow.

Be human

Treat people like people. Tell the truth. Do what you say. And put yourself in the shoes of others.

What's in a name?

Hot dogs and eggs are two simple ingredients hacked together to hit the spot. The opposite of fancy. It's ghetto. And delicious.

The bare minimum. Hot dogs and eggs are just what you need. Nothing more.

Inside this site you'll find an appetite for these beliefs everywhere you look - blog posts, influences, and projects.
