Hi, I'm Chris Gallo.

And this is hotdogsandeggs, where I write about treating customers like humans.

I spend my days making customers feel important at Highrise. We're a small software company that helps people keep track of who they talk to, what they talk about, and when to follow up.

Here's a few things I believe:

  • The best answer when communicating with customers is often a question.
  • Listen to what people do, not just what they say.
  • Support your product like a customer, become a user.

And a few things I do day-to-day:

  • Communicate directly with customers
  • Create onboarding resources
  • Write help documentation
